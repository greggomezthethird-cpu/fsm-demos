Common Questions

What Tolleson homeowners ask most before calling.

How fast can you get here?
Most calls get a same-day visit. We're based right here in the West Valley and keep the schedule flexible for urgent situations. Call now for a straight answer on timing.
What does a typical repair cost?
It depends on the issue — a capacitor replacement is much less than a refrigerant recharge or compressor swap. What doesn't change: you get a firm quote before any work starts. No surprises.
Are you licensed and insured?
ROYS Air Conditioning & Heating is a family-run Tolleson shop. Ask us anything about credentials when you call — we're happy to walk you through it.
What brands do you work on?
All major brands — Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Rheem, Goodman, American Standard, and more. If it heats or cools, we can diagnose and fix it.
Do you offer free estimates?
Yes — free estimates on all new installations and replacements. For repairs, we diagnose the issue on-site and give you a firm price before starting any work.
